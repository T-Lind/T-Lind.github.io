Split Decisions

Overview: Split Decisions is a fast-paced brain teaser where you must answer two questions simultaneously. One question appears on the left panel and one on the right. Each question is randomly generated from a variety of typesâ€”such as math puzzles, prime checks, word scrambles, and more.

How to Play:

  • The left panel uses the keys Q, W, E, R.
  • The right panel uses the keys U, I, O, P.
  • You have 5 seconds per round to answer both questions correctly.
  • If you answer one incorrectly or run out of time, the game is over.
  • Press the Start Game button (or hit Space/Enter) to begin, and use the same keys to restart after a game over.
