The Arcadia Initiative

The really-high-talent organization.

The Arcadia Initiative is an exclusive organization designed for highly talented individuals seeking to work on innovative, cross-disciplinary projects without the bureaucracy of traditional student organizations. It offers a platform for collaboration, funding, and intellectual stimulation, with a focus on programmers, mathematicians, and physicists, though exceptional minds from any discipline are welcome.

Learn more at arcadiainitiative.github.io.
