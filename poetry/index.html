My Poetry

Yes, I write poetry. Some of it's good, some of it's... less good. I write about a variety of topics, and I do my best to keep it apolitical and not promote any one religion in particular. After all, it'd be stupid to publish either one of those positions on the internet. As to the individual subject matter: much of it is impersonal. Much of it is purely fictitious. So don't speculate. Don't assume you know my positions or life circumstances, because you are probably wrong.

It's a good way for me to relax, get my artistic side out, and develop my writing skills.

> You can access my poetry here.

I'm always open to feedback (you can comment on the articles). If you use it elsewhere, please make sure to get my approval and attribute me.
